Boys Hostel

Hostel accommodation will be provided strictly on merit for about 250 students.
All essential facilities are available to the boarders.
AC & Non-AC rooms
24/7 Water supply
24/7 Electricity supply with dedicated 125 KVA Genset for Hostel
Wi-Fi facility
Well-furnished common rooms with TV with DTH Connection
24 Hour Security
Indoor games (Carom,Badminton etc)
Tuck Shops
Air Conditioned Mess providing hygienic food to the boarders.
Ragging is strictly prohibited in or outside the hostels.
